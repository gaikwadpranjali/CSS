*Our Special Bouquts*



  • Roses: The ultimate classic for romance, anniversaries, and luxury, available year-round in almost every color.
  • Peonies:Coveted for their lush, ruffled blooms; highly popular in spring for bridal and high-end events.
  • Lilies: Known for their striking trumpet shape and sweet fragrance; often used for sympathy, weddings, or grand displays.
  • Carnations: Long-lasting and heavily ruffled, bringing texture and surprising elegance to modern designer arrangements.
  • Hydrangeas:Excellent as a "foundation" flower because of their massive, round, and voluminous heads.
